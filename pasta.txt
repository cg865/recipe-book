Ingredients:
250g (9 oz) pasta (penne, fettuccine, or your choice)

2 tbsp butter

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

½ cup milk

1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)

½ tsp black pepper

½ tsp Italian seasoning (optional)

1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
Cook the pasta:

Boil water in a large pot with salt.

Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.

Drain and set aside.

Prepare the creamy sauce:

In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat.

Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

Pour in the heavy cream and milk, stirring constantly.

Add cheese and seasoning:

Stir in the Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning.

Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes until the sauce thickens.

Combine with pasta:

Add the cooked pasta to the pan and mix well.

Stir in the shredded mozzarella cheese until melted and creamy.

Serve and enjoy:

Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan cheese.

Serve warm and enjoy your creamy pasta!
